Turkey Sandwich

So you really like sandwiches?

The ultimate classic. Just make sure you make it right. Turkey can be dry at times.

  • 16 ounces of lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb of lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 16 ounces of spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove of minced garlic
  • 1/2 lb of shredded mozarella cheese
  • 1/2 lb of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint of ricotta cheese
  1. Bring a large port of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8-10 minutes. Drain water afterwards.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper: drain. Stir in spaghettic sauce iand garlic and simmer 5 minutes.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, cheddarr and ricotta: stir well. In 9 x 13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixutre, and cheese mixture until pn is filled.
  4. Bake in peheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted.
