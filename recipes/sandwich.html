Turkey Sandwich

So you really like sandwiches?

The ultimate classic. Just make sure you make it right. Turkey can be dry at times.

  • 2 slices of white or wheat bread
  • 2-4 pieces of sliced turkey breast
  • mayonaise
  • 2 leaves of lettuce
  • (optional) 3 pieces of bacon
  • 2 pieces of your choice of cheese
  • 1 Hell's Kitchen contestant
  • 1 world renowned british chef
  1. Take two slices of bread and apply mayonaise to one end
  2. Slice 2 pieces of tomato and start pan frying your bacon if you choose to have bacon
  3. Place your meat, cheese, tomato slices, and lettuce pieces on the side with mayonaise.
  4. Apply bacon on top and then add the other piece of bread.
