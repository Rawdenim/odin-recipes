Lasagna Recipe

Easy and Delicious Lasagna

This is all you need to start cooking delicious lasagna for your whole family. This recipe is easy, quick, and has plenty of leftovers. There are only 4 major steps!

  • 16 ounces of lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb of lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 16 ounces of spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove of minced garlic
  • 1/2 lb of shredded mozarella cheese
  • 1/2 lb of shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint of ricotta cheese
