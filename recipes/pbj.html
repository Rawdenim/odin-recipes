Peanut Butter and Jelly Recipe

The Most Difficult Dish in the World

Are you ready to earn your first Michelin Star? This dish transcends all cultures and tastes buds. The ultimate sandwich.

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of peanut butter
  • 1 jar of jelly
  • 2 ozs of love
  • 10 cups of hardwork
  • 15 years of experience
  1. Pick up your bag of white bread and then proceed to open the top of the cover. Then pull out 2 slices of bread.
  2. Place your slices of bread on a cutting board or plate. Grab your jar of peanut butter and then twist of the cap. Grab a butter knife with the other hand and then remove as much peanut butter to taste. Transfer the peanut butter from the knife to a side of bread.
  3. Grab a jar of jelly with one hand and then remove the cap with your other hand. Once the cap is removed, place the cap down, grab a butter knife with your free hand, and grab jelly from the tip of your knife. Generously apply the jelly to the other slice of bread that does not have peanut butter.
  4. Put down the jar of jelly. Now grab both slices of bread and put them on top of each other with the jelly and peanut butter side touching each other.
  5. You now have a completed peanut butter and jelly sandwich. Take a bite and feel proud!
